Standard

Accordions should always be wrapped in an accordion_wrapper to ensure proper styling and functionality when multiple accordions are used together.

{%- call ui.util.call(show_example, language="jinja2") -%} {%- raw %} {%- call ui.util.call(ui.accordion_wrapper) -%} {{ ui.accordion(lipsum(1), title="First accordion") }} {{ ui.accordion(lipsum(1), title="Second accordion") }} {%- endcall %} {%- endraw %} {%- endcall %}
