Initially open

By default, accordions are closed when the page loads. However, you can set an accordion to be initially open by passing the open=true argument.

{%- call ui.util.call(show_example) -%} {%- raw %} {%- call ui.util.call(ui.accordion_wrapper) -%} {{ ui.accordion(lipsum(1), title="Initially open accordion", open=true) }} {%- endcall %} {%- endraw %} {%- endcall %}
