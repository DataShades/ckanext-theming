Field info

content of the input(first positional argument) is rendered as an additional text below or above the field. If it contains help text, wrap it into field_info to apply proper styles and icons.

{%- call ui.util.call(show_example) -%} {%- raw %} {{ ui.input(ui.field_info("Help text here"), name="name", label="Name") }} {%- endraw %} {%- endcall %}
